Private Six-Day Tour from Tangier

Embark on a six-day private tour from Tangier, designed to showcase Morocco’s diverse beauty and rich cultural heritage. This immersive journey takes you through stunning landscapes, historic sites, and vibrant cities, offering a perfect balance of adventure and cultural exploration.

About This Trip

This private tour covers some of Morocco’s most enchanting destinations, including Tangier, the blue city of Chefchaouen, the ancient city of Fez, and the Roman ruins of Volubilis. Throughout the trip, you'll experience cultural visits, trekking excursions in the Rif Mountains, guided tours of Moroccan Kasbahs and medinas, and time to explore traditional souks and historical landmarks.

Trip Highlights

  • Tangier: Discover the charm of this coastal city, a gateway between Europe and Africa.
  • Chefchaouen: Stroll through the famous blue-washed streets of this picturesque mountain town.
  • Fez: Explore the cultural and spiritual heart of Morocco, home to historic medinas and traditional markets.
  • Rif Mountains: Enjoy trekking excursions through breathtaking landscapes.
  • Cultural Immersion: Experience local traditions, shop in lively souks, and visit museums and monuments.

This six-day adventure offers a perfect blend of history, nature, and culture, making it an unforgettable journey through Morocco’s most iconic destinations.
