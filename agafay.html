Agafay Desert

The Agafay Desert is a breathtaking rocky desert located just outside of Marrakech. Known for its stunning lunar landscapes, rolling hills, and golden dunes, Agafay offers visitors a unique escape from the city’s hustle. Unlike typical sandy deserts, Agafay’s charm lies in its vast stone plains and serene atmosphere.

Agafay desert Experience

Discover the amazing mesmerizing landscapes of agafay

About This Trip

Just 40 km from Marrakech, the Agafay desert immerses you in a surprising landscape, far from the classic sand dunes. This astonishing stone desert, with its hills and vast expanses, offers an authentic experience for lovers of nature and adventure. Between starry skies, camel rides and meetings with nomads, Agafay is a true haven of peace to discover. Prepare yourself to live unforgettable moments in this hidden gem of Marrakech.

We also provide a short stay in the camp, including the swimming pool, lunch or dinner, and time to enjoy Moroccan music and fireworks.

Trip Highlights

  • Camel and Horse Rides: Experience the thrill of riding a camel or horse through the picturesque desert landscapes, immersing yourself in the beauty of Agafay's unique terrain.
  • Quad Biking Adventures: For an adrenaline rush, embark on a thrilling quad biking adventure, navigating through the rocky paths and enjoying the stunning scenery.
  • Stargazing: At night, Agafay transforms into a stargazer's paradise. Enjoy the clear skies and breathtaking views of the stars, far away from the city lights.
  • Traditional Berber Experiences: Immerse yourself in the local culture by visiting a traditional Berber camp, where you can learn about their customs, enjoy a delicious Moroccan meal, and participate in local music and dance.
  • Relaxing in Luxury Camps: Experience the beauty of the desert while staying in luxurious glamping accommodations, offering comfort and stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

This day trip from Marrakech to Agafay provides a unique blend of adventure, culture, and natural beauty, making it a memorable escape in Morocco's captivating desert landscape.
